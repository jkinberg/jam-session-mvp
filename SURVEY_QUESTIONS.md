## Google Forms Setup Tips

1. **Create sections** to match the organization above
2. **Enable progress bar** for better UX
3. **Required fields:** Questions 4, 5, 6, 8, 9, 10, 14
4. **Optional fields:** Everything else (don't force too much)
5. **Response validation:**
   - Email field should validate email format
   - Keep it user-friendly (no CAPTCHA needed for small test)
6. **Settings:**
   - Collect email addresses: Optional
   - Limit to 1 response: No
   - Show progress bar: Yes
   - Shuffle question order: No
